AutoDesk Maya: Shading, Texturing, Lighting, and Rendering

Shading and Texturing

I used various type of shading and texturing for the elephant. I used lambert for most of the part of the elephant, the body, toes, and trunks. I used Blinn for the eye so it has some reflectivity, I had to lower the specular value to prevent it becoming very light.

Lighting

I used a three-point lighting system using three spot lights to illuminate the elephant. The background also helped in lighting. I added an environment color from a 360 image.

Rendering

To render this I used Arnold Renderer, I made sure that the lights have the right exposure so that Arnold renderer can capture this. On tthe render settings, I set it up to export PNGs for each frame, then imported these photos in Premiere Pro to make this into a video.
